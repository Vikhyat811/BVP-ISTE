Past Events

A look back at our memorable moments.

Generative AI Tools & Applications Session🌐

A hands-on session exploring the latest in Generative AI. Members experimented with various AI tools and learned to apply them in real-world projects, from text generation to image synthesis.

Beyond The Buzzer🕹

Join us for an evening of intellectual fun and competition. The event begins with a general knowledge quiz to test your wits, followed by a final, high-stakes round of Jeopardy to determine our ultimate champion.

Tech Titans💻

18th October,2024
Tech Titans turned the traditional product pitch approach into a dynamic exhibit of ideas for the future. On our stage, more than 20 groups of budding creative minds showcased innovative solutions related to digital transformation, sustainability, and healthcare.

Squid Games!!

16th October, 2024
Our fun-filled take on the popular Netflix show turned traditional kid-friendly games into a memorable strategic and skill-building event. From Red Light Green Light to team-based games, participants from all around the community gathered to put their wits and agility to the test through a variety of creatively recreated obstacles.

UpGrad Elevate Seminar

15th October, 2024
In an engaging and insightful session, participants came together to explore endless possibilities for pursuing higher education at top-tier global universities. From comprehensive guidance by study abroad experts to exclusive scholarship opportunities worth INR 40 lakhs, the seminar was packed with value-driven takeaways.

Hack The Crisis 3.0💻

13th-14th March,2024
HackTheCrisis 3.0 at BVCOE New Delhi was a 24-hour national hackathon that brought together 300+ participants and 60+ teams to build tech-driven solutions for climate change and sustainability. With a prize pool of ₹50K+, teams used AI, IoT, and blockchain to innovate across healthcare, finance, education, and smart cities. The event fostered collaboration, learning, and real-world impact.
